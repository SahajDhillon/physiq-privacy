Physiq Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Physiq ("we", "our", "the app") is a body measurement tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or email/password, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture URL through Firebase Authentication.
  • Body Measurements: Weight, chest, waist, hips, and other measurement data you voluntarily enter.
  • Profile Details: Height, age, gender, fitness goals, and unit preferences you provide.
  • Progress Photos: Photos you choose to upload for tracking your fitness journey.
  • Microphone Audio: When you use the Voice Input feature, your speech is processed on-device to fill measurement forms. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any external server.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (tracking measurements, generating charts, calculating body metrics).
  • To sync your data across devices via your authenticated account.
  • To generate personalized insights and progress summaries.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage). Data is associated with your unique user ID and protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can access your own data. All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Google Sign-In — Account authentication.

6. Permissions

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Used exclusively for the Voice Input feature to convert speech to text on-device. No audio is stored or sent externally.
  • Camera / Photo Library: Used to capture or select progress photos and profile pictures.
  • Internet: Required to sync data with Firebase and authenticate your account.
  • Notifications: Used to send measurement reminder notifications you configure.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your measurement data and photos are visible only to you.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual measurements from within the app at any time. To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

9. Children's Privacy

Physiq is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
